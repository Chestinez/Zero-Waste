My Challenges

Overcoming My Challenges

Starting a zero-waste lifestyle isn't easy. I might struggle with habits, convenience, and availability of eco-options. But I plan to stay committed by tracking progress, staying inspired by zero-waste communities online, and reminding myself that every small step counts.
For example, because most zero-waste commitments are challenges imposed on my daily lives I may easily struggle to endure and adapt to a new lifestyle. Furthermore my zero-waste pursuits may also clash with my family, as my grandmother may just dispose my 30-day trash jar without a further thought.
