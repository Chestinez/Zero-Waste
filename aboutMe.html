About Me

Who I am?

Hello I am Cheston! I am a current year 1 student under SP's diploma in Information Technology.


I am passionate about protecting the planet through mindful choices and minimal waste. I believe even the smallest eco-friendly change can make a big difference. My journey toward a zero-waste lifestyle began with small steps, refusing plastic straws, reusing jars, and composting kitchen scraps. These habits grew into a lifestyle rooted in sustainability and creativity.
